üöÄ How to Use

  1. Download the nature-buttons.css file
  2. Include it in your HTML: <link rel="stylesheet" href="nature-buttons.css">

    • Themes: .sun, .flower, .leaf, .garden
    • Sizes: .small, .large
    • Styles: .primary, .secondary
